Every memory has a place

The Coordinates Collection is the world’s first customizable wearable memory. The brand launches June 2013 in the jewelry vertical with a collection consisting of bracelets and rings. Each piece combines chic sophistication and jet set glamour displaying the latitude and longitude of any place that holds a special memory to you.

The Coordinates collection is fully customizable via Whatareyours where users can register their pieces’ unique serial ID and tie memories to their piece. It is also the official spot to create your coordinates collection item.

  • we'll turn that place in time into a geographic coordinate and create a serial number for your piece

  • choose from a variety of styles, finishes and precious gems and we'll build your custom coordinate piece
