Every memory has a place

The Coordinates Collection is the world’s first customizable wearable memory. Each piece is a combination of chic sophistication and jet set glamour displaying the latitude and longitude of any place that holds a special memory to you.

The Coordinates collection is fully customizable with a variety of choices of metals, gems, and customized inscriptions. Each Coordinates Collection piece has a unique serial ID which can be registered at Whatareyours, which is the official spot to share and discover memories, experiences, and stories from around the world.

  • Choose a memory close to your heart let us know where that memory happened

  • we'll turn that place in time into a geographic coordinate and create a serial number for your piece

  • choose from a variety of styles, finishes and precious gems and we'll build your custom coordinate piece

Whatareyours

Go to Whatareyours.com to share your memories and where they happened! If you already own a Coordinates Collection piece you can register it and unlock additional benefits and rewards.

  • Share your memorable story on our social map and discover your coordinates!
