Shipping Policy

Coordinates Collection provides customers with FREE shipping and FREE repairs within 3 months of purchase on all orders placed from within continental United States. For international orders, we process through a third party service called international checkout.

HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?

We will be able to send you a tracking number once your order has been accepted and your package has been shipped out from our warehouse. Coordinates Collection is unable to provide tracking for international orders unfortunately.

HOW CAN I CHANGE/CANCEL MY ORDER?

Email customer@fd9group.com and let us know your order number and reason for canceling.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO RECEIVE MY ORDER?

Depending on the shipping method and your location (within the continental United States or outside of it) will depend on when you receive your order. As all pieces are customized, it may take 2-3 weeks for your piece to be created. We try our best to deliver products quickly and with high quality control standards!

  • FREE: Standard Shipping (5-7 business days) within continental United States.
  • 2 Business Days & Overnight available (charges apply).

WHAT ARE THE RATES FOR DOMESTIC SHIPPING?

Coordinates Collection offers FREE standard shipping within Continental United States, for 2 business days and overnight different rates apply based on destination.

CAN I PLACE AN INTERNATIONAL ORDER?

For your convenience Coordinates Collection also provides international shipping. Coordinates Collection has partnered with International Checkout for all purchases outside of the continental United States. Please make sure when you purchase from outside of the US to check out by using the International Checkout button. You will automatically be redirected to the International Checkout website to finalize your order. For more information about International Checkout please visit http://www.internationalcheckout.com/cs.php.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO RECEIVE MY INTERNATIONAL ORDER?

Coordinates Collection has partnered with International checkout to provide worldwide shipping. International Checkout will get your items to your doorstep as quickly as possible.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING TRANSIT TIMES

Standard shipping arrives to your address in approximately 8-20 business days. Priority shipping arrives to your address in approximately 4-10 business days. Transit times are estimated and are not guaranteed. We are not responsible for delays by customs or by the store.

WHAT ARE THE RATES FOR INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING?

Coordinates Collection has partnered with International Checkout who applies their shipping costs based on the destination. For more information please visit to www.internationalcheckout.com.

WHAT IF I HAVEN'T RECEIVED MY ORDER YET?

Coordinates Collection works with a third party delivery source, please use the tracking number provided to track your purchase. For international purchasers if you haven’t received your items after 15 days prior to confirmation of shipping please email customer@fd9group.com and we will do our best to assist you in locating it. For International orders please contact International Check Out by visiting https://www.internationalcheckout.com/contact.php.

HOW DO I KNOW THIS IS THE RIGHT ORDER?

The order # should correspond with the product number shown on the packaging.

WHAT DO I DO WHEN THE SIZE, STYLE, COLOR IS WRONG?

If you believe we have sent you the incorrect style that you did not order. First check your online order receipt and make sure that it does not match the receipt in your package. If after that there is a discrepancy, Coordinates Collection will ensure you to receive the correct items (notified via email). Contact us immediately once you are certain that you have received the wrong item. Email customer@fd9group.com with the incorrect size or style that you received and the correct size or style that you desired. Coordinates Collection will confirm and validate your request via email and provide you with a shipping label via email to return the items. Once we have received the incorrect item(s) we will ship the correct item(s). As stated above, Coordinates Collection is not responsible for any goods not received during the return process.

WHAT TO DO WHEN MY ITEM IS DELIVERED DAMAGED?

Coordinates Collection applies an extensive quality control, however if damaged goods do occur. Please contact customer@fd9group.com immediately to request an exchange.

WHAT ARE THE RATES FOR INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING?

Coordinates Collection has partnered with International Checkout who apply their shipping locations based on the destination.

WHAT IF I REFUSE DELIVERY ON AN INTERNATIONAL PACKAGE?

If you refuse delivery on an international order – the package will be abandoned at the border. Because the package will not be received back by us, you will not be refunded for your purchase. Please review international orders very carefully to ensure that you have selected the correct color and size and are willing to accept the duties/taxes and brokerage fees as we cannot cancel an order once it has been placed.

WHO IS RESPONSIBLE FOR DUTIES/TAXES AND BROKERAGE FEES ON AN INTERNATIONAL SHIPMENT?

The recipient of the package is responsible for these fees. These fees are not included in the pricing or shipping/handling charges on our website. It is strongly recommended to check with your local agency for an estimate of these fees prior to placing the order. International Checkout allows you to prepay the DUTIES and TAXES on an international shipment.
